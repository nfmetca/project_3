ABOUT ME

I am a junior at Illinois State University pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design. I am from Lake Zurich, Illinois, a town in the northwest suburb of Chicago. My entire life, I have always been interested in the arts. Outside of design, I enjoy yarn crafts such as knitting and crocheting. I mostly create wearables, but I dabble into other functional pieces as well. I am drawn to design because I strive to create things that are not only visually appealing but also functional and purposeful.
